About & Services


Prime Hoopers

Prime Hoopers is a static web application created and designed by Noli II B. Albay that is developed with the help of different web technologies such as HTML and CSS. The purpose of this project is to let us fully familiarize with different CSS syntax and to develop our creativity in designing web application. On this web application, you can view different articles that are about on the current event on NBA. It is also a recruitment web application that encourages people to join on different pieces of training we offer that will help them improve their skills on basketball court assisting them with our different expert trainers.

Basketball Training Services

Private Skills Training

There is no faster way to improve your game than private skills training. Each session lasts one hour and is packed with specific drills to fit each athlete's individual needs. After a skill evaluation, a personalized program will be created to improve weaknesses and build on strengths. Parents and athletes may also request to focus on certain skills, and the program will be tailored exactly to your needs in each area.

Small Group Training

Taking a similar approach to private skills training, small group training places 3-6 athletes of the same skill level together. Individual skills are still the main focus, but a small group allows for competitive drills and game situations. For example, the pick and roll, high-low situations, and many other game skills can be honed in this setting. Athletes can sign up as a group or be placed by our staff based on age and skill level.

Pricing

Team Training

During a team training session, players will be put through a series of drills to improve their individual skills and also their knowledge of team play. Coaches may request certain skills be worked on or even ask with help drawing up new plays for the team to run in games. Coach Ben Stirt will work side-by-side with the coach to help the team be the best it can be.

Dependent on location and length of practice.
